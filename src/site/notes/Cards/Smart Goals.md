The team sets SMART goals:

- ﻿﻿Specific (not broad)
- ﻿﻿Measurable (so we know when we accomplished them)
- ﻿﻿Attainable (so we ensure we can accomplish them)
- ﻿﻿Relevant (to our work)
- ﻿﻿Timely (has a clear and realistic timeline)
